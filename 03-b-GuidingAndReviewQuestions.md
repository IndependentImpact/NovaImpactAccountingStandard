## Guiding And Review Questions

This catalog replaces appendix framing and is the canonical 03-b question source.

Validation and verification completion is achieved only when:

1. paragraph/anchor-level checks are approved, and
2. mandatory section/document-level qualitative checks are approved.

| Question ID | Mandate | Scope | Linked principles | Question |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| GQ-001 | validation | paragraph | accounting-relevance | Does the stated purpose clearly define the intended positive impact and its link to the target environment? |
| GQ-002 | validation | paragraph | accounting-completeness | Is the justification for the activity purpose complete and supported by evidence or baseline data? |
| GQ-003 | verification | paragraph | accounting-consistency | Is the implemented activity consistent with its original stated purpose? |
| GQ-004 | verification | paragraph | accounting-accountability | Were any material changes to the activity purpose or objectives disclosed and justified? |
| GQ-005 | validation | section | accounting-completeness | Is the section-level narrative internally coherent across purpose, target environment, and intervention details? |
| GQ-006 | verification | section | accounting-accuracy | Does section-level evidence support that implemented interventions match approved section content? |
| GQ-007 | validation | document | accounting-accountability | Does the full PDD present a coherent qualitative argument that all mandatory sections jointly support the claimed impacts? |
| GQ-008 | verification | document | accounting-verifiability | Does cross-section and linked-artifact evidence support the full-document qualitative judgment without unresolved contradictions? |
