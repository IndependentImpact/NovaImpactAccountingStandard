## Glossary

### Document authenticity proof mechanisms

Controlled vocabulary describing how document authenticity is proven.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EdDSA signature | Authenticity proven via an EdDSA digital signature. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/eddsa-signature |
| None | No authenticity proof is provided. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/none |
| RSA signature | Authenticity proven via an RSA digital signature. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/rsa-signature |
| Verifiable credential | Authenticity proven via a verifiable credential. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/vc |

### Impact benefit classification

Controlled vocabulary indicating whether an impact is beneficial or adverse.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Adverse | Impact that has an adverse environmental effect. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/adverse |
| Beneficial | Impact that has a beneficial environmental effect. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/beneficial |

### Impact intentionality

Controlled vocabulary indicating whether an impact was intentional or unintentional.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Intentional | Impact that was deliberately intended. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/intentional |
| Unintentional | Impact that occurred without being deliberately intended. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/unintentional |

### Impact monitoring status

Controlled vocabulary indicating whether an impact will be monitored.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| No | Negative response to a yes/no requirement. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/no |
| Yes | Affirmative response to a yes/no requirement. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/yes |

### Methodology approval status

Controlled vocabulary describing the approval status of a methodology within the Nova Impact Accounting Standard.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Approved | Methodology has passed formal evaluation and is approved for use under the standard. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/approved |
| Deprecated | Methodology is no longer recommended for new projects; existing registered projects may continue to use it. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/deprecated |
| Proposed | Methodology has been submitted for consideration but has not yet entered formal evaluation. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/proposed |
| Rejected | Methodology was evaluated and rejected; it may not be used under the standard. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/rejected |
| Surpassed | Methodology has been superseded by a newer or improved version; projects should migrate to the replacement. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/surpassed |
| Under evaluation | Methodology is currently undergoing formal technical evaluation and review. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/under-evaluation |

### Monitored or fixed ex-ante

Controlled vocabulary indicating whether a data parameter will be monitored during the crediting period or fixed ex-ante.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fixed ex-ante | Parameter or data point whose value is determined and fixed prior to the start of the crediting period. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/fixed-ex-ante |
| Monitored | Parameter or data point whose value is determined through ongoing monitoring during the crediting period. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/monitored |

### Report status

Controlled vocabulary describing the lifecycle status of report content and impact estimates (projected, measured, verified, revised).

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Measured | Report content expresses values measured from real observed states. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/measured |
| Projected | Report content expresses projected or forecast values that are not yet measured. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/projected |
| Revised | Report content has been revised after initial publication. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/revised |
| Verified | Report content has been independently verified and attested. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/verified |
