KSR can also be lost through time-based decay (more details are provided in the 'Time-based decay' section below)


### Time-based decay

KSR decays  at a rate of X points every Y months. Decay stops the moment that a reputation balance reaches zero, i.e., it cannot drop below zero due to decay. 

Decay incentivises agents to remain active on the platform and to keep their knowledge and skills up to date. Elaborate.
