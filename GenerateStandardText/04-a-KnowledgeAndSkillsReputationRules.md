## Reputation Lifecycle Terms

### Reputation lifecycle terms

Controlled vocabulary of terms describing the lifecycle dynamics of an agent's Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) on the Nova Impact Accounting Standard platform, including how reputation is gained, how it decays over time, and the parameters that govern that decay.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Decay floor | The minimum Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) balance to which time-based decay can reduce an agent's score; decay stops when this floor is reached and cannot cause a negative balance. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/decay-floor |
| Decay rate | The configurable rate at which a platform agent's Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) balance decreases per unit of time when the agent is inactive. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/decay-rate |
| KSR balance | The current total of a platform agent's Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) points in a given domain, calculated as cumulative gains minus cumulative decay, with a floor of zero. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/ksr-balance |
| KSR decay | A periodic, time-based reduction in a platform agent's Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) balance, applied at a configurable rate to incentivise continued platform engagement and knowledge currency. Decay stops when the balance reaches zero. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/ksr-decay |
| KSR gain | An increase in a platform agent's Knowledge & Skills Reputation (KSR) balance, earned by submitting reviews that are accepted by the platform. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/ksr-gain |
