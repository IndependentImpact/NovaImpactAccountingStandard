## Requirement Status

### Requirement status

Controlled lifecycle statuses for VVS requirement resources.

| Term | Definition | IRI |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Active | The requirement is formally adopted and currently in force. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/vvs/active |
| Deprecated | The requirement has been superseded and is no longer in force. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/vvs/deprecated |
| Draft | The requirement is under development and has not yet been formally adopted. | https://nova.org.za/novaimpactaccountingstandard/vvs/draft |
